Coopers Croft Bacchus

This Bacchus begins with an elderflower nose and then emanates typical Sauvignon notes of zesty citrus fruit. Very elegant and delicate with a slight spritz and a long, clean finish.

Coopers Croft Pinot Noir Rose

A Pinot Noir that is bright and lively and delivers soft subtle aromas of violet and classic strawberry notes. Producing an elegant wine that is cool, crisp and dry. Best served chilled and enjoyed soon after release.

Our vines are located on a South facing slope with underlying chalk which brings character to the wine

Bottled by The Winery Thurston Place Bury St. Edmunds, Suffolk. IP31 3QZ
